Help build an open 3D map of the natural world

Trek View are using crowdsourced street-level imagery to create a virtual world everyone can explore.

Capture imagery

Use any GoPro camera to capture imagery of outdoor environments. Max and Fusion 360 cameras are the best, but any HERO camera will work well.

Create a 3D world

Your imagery is split into frames, placed on a map, and used a to create a 3D model of your adventure.

Explore the natural world

Share your 3D models with the entire world. Anyone can browse publicly available content.

Browse 3D imagery uploaded by fellow trekkers.
